ABOUT US

Paradox 6 Investments was established in 2017 and registered in terms of the South Africa Company Registration Act. Paradox 6 Investments (Pty) Ltd otherwise referred to as P6 E&I, offers dynamic Electrical Engineering, Control and Instrumentation services as the core of the business. P6 E&I’s core then tie’s-in with other multidisciplinary Engineering services in order to complete project objectives successfully. P6 E&I is a proudly owned South African Small to Medium Enterprise (SME) that offers the following engineering services:

• Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC/M) in Electrical Engineering, Control, Instrumentation.
• Multi-discipline Engineering consulting tie-ins (Electrical, Control and Instrumentation)
• Reverse Engineering (As-Building) in Electrical, Control, Instrumentation Automation.
• FEED (Front End Engineering and Design) in Electrical Engineering, Control, Instrumentation.
• Compliance Audits and GAP Analysis in in Electrical Engineering, Control, Instrumentation.
• Project Management, Construction Management

These services are offered to businesses in both the public and private sector, including enterprises (SOE’s), Real estate, Management Companies, Power Stations, Chemicals (i.e., Explosives, Fertilizers), Petrochemical industry, the Steel industry, Renewable Energy Sector, Mining, Water Treatment plants, Municipalities and Hospitals.

Ownership and Management

Paradox 6 Investments is a 100% black owned SMME that aims to create equal employment opportunities for all. This will ensure employment equity and achieve a diverse workplace, as a reflection of the country's demographic. P6 E&I places a high emphasis on good business ethics, sharing of knowledge, the empowerment of individuals from previously disadvantaged communities through personal development and mentorship.
Paradox 6 Investments is a B-BBEE LEVEL 1 Contributor: 135% Procurement Recognition. P6 E&I is currently managed by professionals who have a combined experience of more than 20 years in electrical, control and instrumentation engineering projects. The diverse team puts P6 E&I in a position to execute their core competencies and complex multi-discipline projects.
